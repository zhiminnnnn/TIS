## Reflection
>A significant amount of information regarding the use of technology at PPG was provided during the talk. Support for various systems across several countries is maintained through the utilization of IT by the Data Analytics, Cloud Infrastructure, and SAP COE teams. Within the Data Analytics segment, the nature of data in real-world scenarios was detailed, while the types of cloud services utilized for service delivery were explained in the Cloud Infrastructure portion.
>
>Valuable insights into the interconnection of various business aspects through a single system were gained from the SAP segment. The critical role played by technology in the overall success of a Multi-National Corporation (MNC) was highlighted, and the necessity of connecting disparate business functions was discussed.
>
>It was further explained that the scope of IT extends far beyond software development to include data engineering, cloud management, ERP, and cybersecurity. The importance of acquiring expertise in essential skills, such as SQL, cloud computing, and problem-solving, was emphasized. Furthermore, professional development and career readiness are fostered through exemplary growth initiatives at PPG, such as mentorship programs and global projects for interns.
>
>The talk was found to be highly captivating, as the application of university-acquired knowledge within a real business environment was clearly demonstrated. Consequently, further interest in various IT sectors was stimulated, and motivation for future industry internships was strengthened.
